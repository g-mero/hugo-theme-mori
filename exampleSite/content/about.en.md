## About This Theme

**Mori** is a minimal Hugo theme. It focuses on simplicity and readability, and is easy to internationalize (i18n).

### Features

- Clean and elegant design
- Responsive layout
- Easy to customize
- Fast and lightweight
- Multilingual support (i18n)

### Philosophy

Less is more. This theme believes in the power of simplicity and focuses on what matters most: your content.

### Get Started

Visit the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/g-mero/hugo-theme-mori) to learn more about installation and customization.
